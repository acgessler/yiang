ASCII is back.


... is a crazy Jump'n'Run game featuring an unique text art look.



It offers an epic campaign with 72 levels, dozens of different enemies, small brainteasers and challenging achievements (20+ of them. Chuck Norris earned them all). The game is painfully difficult *and* it has an online highscore ranking - so you won't waste your time for nothing. Make people remember your name or don't, the decision is left to YOU.

Please do not try this game if you are a coward.
